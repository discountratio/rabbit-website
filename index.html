About

Penelope Park is an artist whose work is inspired by the Korean tradition of porcelain painting. She has exhibited her work in galleries and museums around the world, and has been featured on television programs including National Geographic.

Penelope was born in Montreal and grew up in Toronto. At 17, she went to Korea to live with her grandmother for three years, where she learned how to make art with porcelain. Upon returning to Canada and graduating high school, she pursued a career in art.

She started out as a graphic designer, but soon found that she had an affinity for ceramic sculpture. She decided to pursue ceramics as a full-time career and enrolled at Sheridan College's School of Art & Design in Oakville, Ontario.
